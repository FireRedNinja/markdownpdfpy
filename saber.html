xFSN ADC Guide

TeamFighting

  1. Default: Play to Live
  2. Hit the closest Target
  3. Look for 90% Plays (Plays that will work at least 90% of the time)

Lane Matchups Pt. 1: Trade vs Catch

  • Categorise the matchup pre-level 6, so you know what to do in lane

    • Poke
    • Catch
    • Trade
      • Usually have sustain
    • All-in

  • Supports are the most important part of laning

  • In trade vs catch

    • you want to trade

How do we trade?

  • trade with them whenever they want something

    • most obvious, when they want a minion
      • best trades are on a ranged minion and cannon
    • when they are locking up for no reason

  • keep lane in the middle to give you good opportunity to trade

  • you trade when there is a reason to trade

Priority

  • dont get hit by their "catch"

  • your cs

  • use our category, trade

  • make sure you know what to do before something happens

  • if you have a trade matchup and they dont, you want to trade

  • if they have a category that you dont have, dont do it

  • if you do your category better than your opponent you will win

Lane Matchups Pt. 2: Catch vs Trade

  • try to avoid getting pressured on last hits and set up the wave to let the support to catch

  • we don't do anything until the support catches or I catch and my support follows

  • we are not starting anything unless we have matchup related ideas

  • to help support catch better, we want enemy to come to middle

    • so freeze

  • if you have flash, you will be ok from jungler gank if you don't trade while you are pushing

    • after you push, don't trade and run from the jungler

Lane Matchups Pt. 3: All-in vs Trade

  • don't try to trade

    • don't walk up if you don't have a reason to

  • take the cs and back off

  • don't try to do anything too crazy until you can all-in

  • you can play around the jungle, in the sense that you can still flash out if the jungle shows up

  • If you are trying to go for an all in, make sure there isn't a gank incoming

  • figure out ways to stop trading with you

  • figure out ways to make an all

How to get more all-ins

  • if you clear the ward at tri-bush you could camp at lane bush for an all-in
  • clear ward at tri-bush and channel base for a second then camp the tri or lane bush

Mechanics: Counterplay

  • Counterplay - they have a way to kill you and you have a way to beat it
  1. Know how they could kill you?
  2. How can I beat it?
    • Be specific on how your counterplay will run

If you can execute, go for it

Two Tips to Help You Win on ADC

  • These are rules, and when you break them, you are expected outcome is a loss
  • Rules are meant to be broken, you must have a good reason
  1. Do not hit tower
    • When you hit tower you are vulnerable to engages
  2. Do not commit to a doomed fight
    • Numbers disadvantage
    • First thing you do before a fight breaks out, look at minimap
    • Don't start a doomed fight either
    • Don't commit until it looks good
